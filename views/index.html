INDÛ is a Kapampangan word meaning "mother" or profoundly means "where you came from" (Indung Ibatan). A local apparel brand started 2014 & the concept is to promote local & homegrown goods which is well known for its premium quality affordable crewneck shirts proudly made in Angeles City.
